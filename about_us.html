Our Story

Our Story

How a simple idea grew into a platform transforming internship experiences for students and employers alike.

From Classroom Frustration to Industry Solution

Growtern was founded in 2018 by a group of university students who struggled to find quality internships that matched their skills and career aspirations. Frustrated by the disconnect between academic preparation and real-world opportunities, they set out to create a better solution.

What began as a campus index quickly gained traction, expanding to serve students at over 500 universities nationwide. Today, Growtern is the fastest-growing internship platform, recognized for our innovative matching algorithm and commitment to equitable access.

Our mission remains the same: to eliminate barriers between talented students and meaningful professional experiences that launch careers.
